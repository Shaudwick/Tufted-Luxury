The Art of Tufting

At Black Lobby, we combine traditional tufting techniques with modern design to create exceptional rugs that transform spaces. Our process begins with carefully selected materials - from premium wool to luxurious silk - chosen for their durability, texture, and visual appeal.

Each rug is handcrafted by skilled artisans who have mastered the art of tufting. Using specialized tufting guns, they carefully insert yarn through a primary backing material, creating loops or cut piles that form the rug's surface. This meticulous process allows for precise control over pile height, density, and pattern.

After tufting, each rug undergoes a rigorous finishing process. We apply a secondary backing for stability, trim the surface to ensure even pile height, and perform thorough quality checks. The result is a handcrafted masterpiece that combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.
